ADVANCE PRO BONO

Advance pro-bono is a national, non-profit consulting firm that brings strategic thinking and results-oriented analysis to the leaders of non-profits in the UK.

We work to build a better world by helping nonprofit organizations achieve breakthrough results. We bring strategic thinking and results-oriented analysis to the leaders of organizations dedicated to the charity sector. In serving our clients, we create meaningful professional development opportunities for our volunteers, and provide our partner firms a vehicle for positive community engagement.

We work to empower our clients with actionable, insightful results and provide our volunteers unparalleled leadership opportunities through these rewarding experiences. In the process, we hope to define what it means to provide impact in community engagement.

Advance pro-bono is run entirely by volunteers, consisting of members of leading management consulting firms. These volunteers organize into teams of roughly five members to provide targetted assistance to local non-profit organizations in addition to their day jobs.

What we do

Advance pro-bono offers management and strategy consulting services to nonprofit organizations and schools. Through the use of data-driven tools, Advance pro-bono seeks to help leaders of nonprofit organizations make critical decisions about the future of their organization.

Areas of Advance pro-bono expertise

Advance pro-bono has functional expertise in a variety of areas, including:

  • Growth and expansion strategy
  • Organizational effectiveness
  • Marketing / publicity
  • Impact assessment
  • New program development
  • Financial planning and modeling
  • Revenue enhancement
  • Volunteer experience improvement

Cases

What kind of output can I expect from an Advance pro-bono Pro Bono case?

Advance pro-bono projects are designed to significantly impact our client organizations, specifically around issues of strategy and organizational assessment. Projects typically create a series of presentations that communicate insights and recommendations to the organization. Many Advance pro-bono cases involve surveys to key stakeholders and constituents and these results are shared throughout the duration of the project. As a result, the final presentation should be a summary of the recommended courses of action that have emerged throughout the project.

What is the difference between working with Advance pro-bono and one of its sponsoring firms?

As a completely volunteer-run organization of junior consultants, Advance pro-bono takes on much smaller projects than the pro bono work of our sponsoring firms. How long are Advance pro-bono cases?

Typical Advance pro-bono cases run approximately 3-4 months, although timing is flexible and Advance pro-bono case teams often extend or shorten timelines, depending on the particular project.

How much do Advance pro-bono cases cost?

Advance pro-bono charges a nominal fee to cover transportation, printing, and other small case team expenses. Since the success of Advance pro-bono cases is driven by the commitment and collaboration by the client, these minimal costs are also used to establish a sense of client investment. Advance pro-bono is sensitive to the financial situations of nonprofit organizations and is open to negotiating fees.
